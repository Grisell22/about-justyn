ISSUES IN MY NEIGHBORHOOD

I live on West Farms Sq and one of the main problems in my neighborhood is that it is so isolated.

My neighborhood contains many forms of transportation but there are not many things to do around there. The only thing that is around is the Bronx Zoo other than that there are only small parks and small fast food places.

I think that some healthier food options should be made in my neighborhood. I also think that more clothings stores, pharmacies and recreational places should be made around my area as well. Places like movie theaters, bowling alleys, dance studios, etc. There are no places for bikes or people to go running. To get a basic necessity for your home you have to drive somewhere to get it or walk for a very long time.
