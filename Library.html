The view over yankee stadium in the Bronx

This area of the bronx contains many parks, a baseball stadium , restaurants and many sources of transportation. There are also two malls and a movie theater, because of this people visit this area more than places like West Farms

The view of the West Farms sq train station

West Farms is small and its main attraction is the Bronx zoo and the Botanical Garden. Other than that there are only small parks and small businesses like pizza shops and delis.There should be more things to do around there and healthier food options. But, this area has a lot of different transportation options

The Mott Haven area in the Bronx

Mott Haven is located in the south western part of the Bronx. There are many public housing buildings and many forms of transportation.

Upper West Side

The Upper West Side is a place with many residents and extremely high rent. Everyone wants to live in this area because the area is always clean and the tourist around make it diverse. There are parks, movie theaters, restaurants ,many forms of transportation and events. There is a strong sense of community and beautiful scenery in this part of New York City.

Washington Heights

In Washington Heights there are many food places and a variety of restaurants. There are parks that host events, pools and other recreational activities. There is a strong sense of community especially with hispanics and the rent is low compared to other places in Manhattan.
